I am a Licensed Professional Counselor (LPC) that has experience in both individual and group counseling in the mental health and substance abuse fields. I have experience in out-patient counseling and school counseling. I have worked with adolescents, young adults, and adults for 15 years and realize that each family is unique, and that treatment needs to be individualized.
I am a young, energetic, and professional woman that enjoys working in the mental health and substance abuse field. I am looking for clients who have come to a crossroad in their life and need to make some behavioral changes and are just unsure how to do so.
I am a cognitive-behavioral counselor that believes you have to change the way you think before you can change the way you act. My approach to counseling involves assessing the problem, defining the goals, and taking action to help you regain control and enhance the quality of your life.
